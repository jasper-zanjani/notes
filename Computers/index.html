    NAS

    • Helios64 is billed as the ultimate ARM-powered NAS. It comes with its own UPS which can power the unit for 15 minutes. It was reviewed by Alex from the Self-Hosted podcast, who took issue with poor build quality and value. Consumer-friendly products from Synology and QNap may perhaps be a better solution.
    • Synology 1621+ was reviewed in Self-Hosted. Synology offers DSM for easy management.
    • Brian Moses has an annual blog series on making your own DIY NAS.
